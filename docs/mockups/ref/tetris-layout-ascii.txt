STRUCTURE SUMMARY
-----------------
1. Header: game title + score + level + lines + Pause + New game.
2. Main: playfield (10x20 grid) with optional Hold and Next panels beside it; score/level/lines in sidebar or under Next.
3. Input: keyboard (arrows/WASD, rotate, hold); optional touch controls.
4. Footer: minimal links.

ELEMENT NOTES
-------------
- Playfield: 10 columns (standard), 20 visible rows; piece spawns at top center.
- Hold: one tetromino; swap current piece with held (once per drop).
- Next: one or more upcoming pieces (1-5 depending on variant).
- Score/Level/Lines: primary stats; level often increases speed.

COMPARISON TO play2048

| Element   | Tetris                    | play2048.co     |

STRUCTURE SUMMARY
-----------------
1. No traditional nav: optional top strip (newsletter, quirky counter/widget).
2. Hero: single line, casual voice ("games and stuff by Neal").
3. One big game grid: card = image + title only; no descriptions; many columns.
4. No "View all" or section headings; grid is the primary content.
5. Footer: first-person one-liner + obligatory links (newsletter, social, support, contact, privacy).

TAKEAWAYS FOR URSA MINOR
------------------------
- Minimal chrome: no mega-nav; optional small strip (e.g. newsletter, light widget).
- One hero line in casual, personal voice (we can do "games under the stars" tone).
- Grid-first: image + title per game; dense and scannable; no card copy needed.
- Personal footer: one sentence + links (blog, Twitter, support, contact, privacy).
- Contrast with NYT: fewer sections, no "latest/feature" block, no "learn more" column.
- Align with night-sky motif: calm, minimal, no purple gradients; grid can feel like a constellation.
